Our Club

UCLA's Society of Physics Students (SPS) is both an academic and social club where students from various disciplines who are interested in physics or physics-related activities come together to have fun and learn new things.

Get Involved: Regualer Meetings

We meet on Tuesday evening from 6-7 pm in Knudsen 2-222 during ODD weeks (week 1, 3, 5...) of the Fall, Spring, and Winter quarters. Free food is provided. There is nothing to sign up for; everyone, including non-physics majors, is welcome! Join our mailing list to get updates about upcoming club events.
